The Hereford Civic Museum

The Hereford Civic Museum is home to the City's past, housing Hereford's historic silver and ancient charters as well as many other artifacts, all of which serve as a link to the City's history. Behind every piece is a story to be told, from our charters stretching back to 1189, to the Silver Cup Trophy from a motor race in 1904, the history of Hereford is here to be told again.
The Civic Museum is entirely FREE to visit, and is open 10.30 am - 3.30 pm on Wednesdays. It is also open to bookings. Please phone 01432 260438 or email mayor@herefordcitycouncil.gov.uk to arrange a visit.
